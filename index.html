SHOPPING APP

The online shopping application of a large retail store offers a user-friendly interface, an extensive product catalog, advanced search and filtering options, secure payment methods, personalized recommendations, customer reviews, order tracking, easy returns and refunds, promotions and discounts, and reliable customer support. It aims to provide a convenient and satisfying online shopping experience for customers.

!

Overview

The application has two modes: light and dark. The content of the application consists of two entities: customers and admins. For admins, there will be a management website with basic functions such as adding, editing, deleting, and viewing the product list, statistics, changing passwords, and managing users, push notification. For customers, they can view the product list, place orders, make purchases, change passwords, edit personal information, and view invoices.
